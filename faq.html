FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What is eChannelling?

    eChannelling is a service that offers convenient booking facilities for Doctor Appointments at island wide prominent hospitals in Sri Lanka. eChannelling has a network of over 200 hospitals with more than 5,500 doctors around the country.

  • What hospitals are available with eChannelling?

    Please refer under HOSPITAL NETWORK

  • How to download eChannelling Mobile Application?

    The Mobile Application can be downloaded via Google play store or App store.

  • How to book an appointment with a doctor?

    Select the preferred date (required session) of the doctor Enter patient details Select the preferred payment method and proceed. You have maximum of 15 minutes to complete the transaction before expiring the appointment.

  • Can I make payments in cash if I channel through the bank?

    Yes Please go to the nearest Sampath bank branch, Peoples bank branch or NDB Bank branch and channel the doctor of your choice. You can make payments in cash to the teller and obtain a receipt for your channel appointment. If you have an account at Sampath bank, Peoples bank, you can also charge the payment to your account with the bank.

  • How do I cancel an appointment that I have made on the internet or at the bank?

    You cannot cancel an appointment made through e-Channelling and obtain a refund unless you have applied for No Show Refund (Please refer terms and conditions of No Show Refund option) However, you could request from the hospital a change of the appointment before the commencement of the doctor's consultation session at the hospital. You may call the hospital to enquire and then visit their channelling counter to request the cancellation. (Telephone number of the hospital is given in your channel receipt)

  • If I misplace or delete the booking confirmation SMS, how can I get the SMS back?

    You can search the appointment details from channel History function in echannelling web site OR Speak to eChannelling customer care center via (0710225225) to get the SMS to your phone free of charge

  • Can I visit the doctor with an Advance Channelling Booking ?

    No. You are required to settle the balance amount, 30 minutes prior to the session time. Failing to settle payment before the session time will result in your appointment being cancelled.
